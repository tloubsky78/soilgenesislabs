Mechanotransduction

Fungi and associated soil organisms respond to mechanical vibrations through mechanosensory pathways (e.g., cell-wall sensors and stretch-activated ion channels). We design stimulation around these biological response modes rather than chemical forcing.

Mycorrhizal Enhancement

By supporting mycorrhizal networks‚Äîthe symbiosis between fungi and plant roots‚Äîwe target improved nutrient access, water handling, and stress resilience (validated by measurement, not assumed).

Ecosystem Restoration

Healthy fungal networks underpin soil aggregation and ecosystem function. Our approach aims to help re-establish these networks in degraded soils through non-destructive stimulation.
